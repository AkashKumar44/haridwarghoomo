Famous Places

Must Visit Places in Haridwar for Pilgrimag

Daksh Mandir

Daksh Mahadev mandir is the place where Parvati Mata became Sati and left her life in Sati Kund.

Mansa Devi

Maa Mansa Devi is a form of Shakti and has emerged from Lord Shiva's mind.

Chandi Devi

The Chandi Devi Temple of Haridwar is a charming temple dedicated to the Chanda Devi Goddess.

Maya Devi

Maya Devi Temple is a popular ancient temple in Haridwar and one of Siddh Peeth located in Uttarakhand.

Har Ki Pauri

It is considered to be the spot where the drops of Amrit fell from the sky, while being carried in a pitcher by the celestial bird, Garuda after the Samudra Manthan. Every day, Har Ki Pauri ghat witnesses hundreds taking a dip in water of the Ganges. The place is considered very auspicious.
